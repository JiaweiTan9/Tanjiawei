Wingsuit flying

Wingsuit flying involves a lot of high technology

  • Wing material

    Stamped and inflated airbags make up the wings. Among them, the airbag is made of nylon, which is very tough and not easily damaged. It's like a bat's wings, very strong.

  • Flying skills

    When in the air, the challenger can use his hands and feet to control the wing membrane to expand and generate buoyancy. So you can control your body in the air.

  • Design principle

    Increase the windward area of the human body to increase the lift, and then realize the unpowered flight of the human in the air.
